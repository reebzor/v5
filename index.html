About Me

My name is Tom Reeb. I live in Philadelphia, PA and I currently work as the Manager of IT Infrastructure at Coriell Institute for Medical Research. My passion for IT stretches beyond my career, far into my personal life; this site is where the two worlds converge. The obvious purpose of this site is to act as an interactive web-based resume, however the driving force behind building it was to further increase my experience with the underlying technologies that power the site itself. I recently migrated this site from a basic LAMP stack on CentOS 6.5 to Nginx on CentOS 7. My main reason for the change was that I was beginning to feel complacent with Apache and EL6. When EL7 was first released I struggled to understand the changes, which only motivated me to learn more. Nginx seems to be better at serving up static web content compared to Apache and is also widely used, experience with it is something that I did not have much of. Minor enhancements such as using HAProxy for SSL termination and MariaDB in favor of MySQL also took place during my migration. After the initial set up, I am much more confident in these technologies, but know I still have a significant amount to learn. In an effort to step up my DevOps game, I am now managing my code on my GitHub and have automated the deployment of this site to production. In fact, this edit was pushed using the auto deploy script!

Head down to my resume section to learn about my skills and experience and check out my social media profiles if you wish to know more about me personally. If you feel as though I may be a qualified candidate for a job, or you would just like to connect with me feel free to contact me. Thanks for stopping by!
